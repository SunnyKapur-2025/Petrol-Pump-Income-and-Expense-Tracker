🏛️ GST & Income Tax

Tax Note for Petrol Pump Owners

Petrol & Diesel are outside GST — they attract VAT + Central Excise. However, CNG/LPG attract GST. Expenses like maintenance, equipment, and services attract GST (Input Tax Credit possible). Income Tax is applicable under Section 44AE / 44AD for presumptive taxation or regular books based on turnover.
